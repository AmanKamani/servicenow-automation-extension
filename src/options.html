What are Search Wait & Dropdown Retries?

Search Wait — After typing into a dropdown, ServiceNow makes an AJAX call to search. This is how long (in ms) to wait for results to appear. Slow fields (like member lookup) may need 5000–10000ms. Max: 60 seconds.

Dropdown Retries — After the search wait, the extension polls for dropdown items to click. Each retry waits ~400ms. If results are slow to render, increase this. e.g. 15 retries = ~6s of polling after the initial wait.

Total max wait = Search Wait + (Retries × 400ms)
e.g. 10000ms wait + 30 retries = 10s + 12s = 22s total
